Our Featured Project
Computer lab for pupils in Masis

We live in times when IT has become ubiquitous. To succeed in this century our younger generation must be not just a mere consumer of technology products but a creator of those. The goal of this project is to empower our younger generation to become creators. To start with we are going to open a computer lab in Masis - a town near Yerevan. For that purpose we need your help in getting PCs for the lab. Individuals and companies can contribute their used computers and we will manage to set up those computers in the lab. If we get more than 10 computers we will reserve the rest for our next project in our next town or village. When the centre is set up and ready, distance learning will become possible. Online teaching can be done both through real-time and recorded lessons. Teachers will be volunteers and the curriculum will be fun and interactive. Contact us now to make a contribution!
